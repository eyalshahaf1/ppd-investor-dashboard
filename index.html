Partner triangle

Design employer

Back-office intensive, measurable workflow data, senior HR and CFO sponsorship.

Regulated rail partner

Insurer, trust bank, payroll, DC, or benefits partner executes under existing infrastructure.

Assurance partner

Validates baseline integrity, evidence samples, exclusion of layoff gains, and reporting consistency.

Risk board

Measurement trust High

Publish a rigorous method, net AI costs, apply confidence haircuts, and use third-party verification.

Regulatory overreach High

Do not touch custody, payments, investment advice, or pension administration. Generate instructions only.

Employer resistance Med

Sell retention, responsible AI legitimacy, and CFO-grade reporting before selling a public mission.

Copycat incumbents Med

Build a benchmark dataset, certification mark, coalition trust, and partner sequencing advantage.

Candidate partner roles

Sompo-like insurer

Credibility, wellbeing narrative, retirement-security distribution, and employer access.

SoftBank-like sponsor

AI ecosystem relevance, strategic capital, and portfolio pilot channels.

METI / MHLW observers

Policy relevance after the measurement method is proven. Observer first, sponsor later.

Best-practice investor demonstration

Run this app like a proof of operating discipline. The point is not to dazzle with big TAM; it is to show that the model survives scrutiny.

Recommended demo sequence

1

Open with the wedge

"We are not replacing Japan's pension system. We are the measurement, allocation, and reporting layer for responsible AI gains."

2

Show the operating flow

Walk left to right: AI workflow, verified gain, dividend rule, regulated rails, impact reporting.

3

Stress-test the math live

Change dividend rate from 5% to 3%, apply a confidence haircut, then show the retirement pool and platform revenue still remain legible.

4

Separate value buckets

Repeat that retirement contributions are worker value. Startup revenue is SaaS, verification, setup, and a modest flow fee.

5

Make risk feel managed

Show the risk board and emphasize measurement integrity, privacy, anti-layoff guardrails, and regulated partner execution.

6

Close with the partner triangle

Ask for two pilots, one regulated rail partner, and one assurance partner to validate a 90-day proof of concept in Japan.

Do and avoid

Do

Use conservative assumptions, show formulas, invite challenge, name the regulated partners, and keep policy upside as optionality.

Avoid

Do not imply custody, guaranteed returns, investment advice, national legislation dependency, or that layoff savings count as eligible gains.

Investor close

"If a 90-day pilot can produce a verified PPD report with real workflow data and a regulated rail partner, then we have the seed of a defensible responsible-AI infrastructure company."
